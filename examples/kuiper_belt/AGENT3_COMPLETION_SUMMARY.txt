WHAT THIS ANALYSIS DOES
================================================================================

Core Function:
  Analyzes clustering patterns in the longitude of ascending node (Ω) for
  Trans-Neptunian Objects with semi-major axis > 100 AU to detect signatures
  of undiscovered distant planets.

Key Capabilities:
  ✓ Circular statistics with proper 360° angle handling
  ✓ Rayleigh test for statistical significance
  ✓ Sub-population analysis (4 distinct populations)
  ✓ Cluster identification and characterization
  ✓ Planet longitude estimation with confidence levels
  ✓ Comprehensive multi-level reporting

Scientific Goal:
  Test the hypothesis that distant TNOs show non-random Ω clustering
  consistent with perturbation by a massive external body (Planet Nine)
